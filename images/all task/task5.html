Burger

A burger, short for hamburger, is a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty, usually beef, placed between two halves of a bun. It's a popular and versatile dish, often served with various toppings and condiments.

Meet Box

A "meat box" typically refers to a curated selection of meat products, often delivered or sold in a subscription or one-time purchase. It's essentially a box containing various cuts of meat, like a custom grocery delivery for meat enthusiasts.
